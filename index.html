About

Designing the logic behind player motivation.

My work sits at the intersection of systems design, economy, progression, UX, and product thinking. I enjoy translating vague design goals into clear loops, balancing structures, and player-facing experiences that are easy to learn and satisfying to optimize.

  • I’ve worked on mobile management, simulation, monster collection, and arcade RPG titles.
  • I’m comfortable moving between concept ideation, gameplay loop design, balance sheets, onboarding tuning, and implementation support in Unity.
  • My academic background in personalization and game-based learning gives me a stronger lens on player behavior and motivation.
